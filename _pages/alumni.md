        <h2 class="my-3">Alumni Network</h2>
        <p>
            The Alumni Group of the MSc in Translational Engineering in Health and Medicine at NTUA is designed to support continued connection and engagement beyond the completion of the program.<br>
            It serves as a professional network where graduates can stay in contact with former classmates, connect with fellow alumni, exchange ideas, share career and research opportunities, and foster collaborations across disciplines and sectors.<br>
            The group aims to strengthen the MSc community by encouraging lifelong professional relationships, knowledge exchange, and active participation in the evolving ecosystem of health and medical engineering.<br>
            All graduates of the program are warmly invited to <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/groups/13323126/"  target="_blank">join</a>, contribute, and remain part of the MSc network.
