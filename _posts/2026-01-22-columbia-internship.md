The NTUA TEAM MSc Program will offer selected students the opportunity to undertake internships at Columbia University (CU), New York, with the support of the Athens Columbia Global Center and the Bodossakis Foundation.

The internships will take place during the summer period and will have a duration of two (2) months (June 15-August 15). Research areas include, but are not limited to, Medical Imaging, Biomechanics, Tissue Engineering, and Neuroengineering.

Interested students are invited to express their interest by sending an email to <a href = "mailto:masterteam-info@ece.ntua.gr">masterteam-info@ece.ntua.gr</a>, including:

* a recent CV (maximum 2 pages), and
* a statement of purpose (maximum 1 page) indicating their preferred research area.

Expressions of interest must be submitted by 20 February 2026.
