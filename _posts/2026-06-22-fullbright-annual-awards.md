Asad Baig, a Drexel University graduate and current MSc TEAM student, shared his experience as a Fulbright Scholar at the Fulbright Annual Awards Ceremony, held on June 18, 2026, at the American School of Classical Studies at Athens. Asad was the only speaker representing the 2025–2026 Fulbright community of students in Greece.

As an MSc TEAM student, Asad spoke about his journey in the program, highlighting how its interdisciplinary environment — spanning engineering, biomedical innovation, and healthcare translation — shaped his experience and supported his growth, opening doors to opportunities such as the StartSmart program that aims to empower the next generation of deep-tech entrepreneurs across South Eastern Europe. His presence at the ceremony is a testament to the caliber of students the MSc TEAM program attracts and its commitment to preparing international students for impact beyond the classroom.

We warmly congratulate Asad and wish him every success in his future academic endeavours. We are also grateful to the Fulbright Program for its invaluable partnership, which makes opportunities like this possible and enriches our academic community.
